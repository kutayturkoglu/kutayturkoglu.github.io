About me

I am an AI researcher and Data Scientist specializing in 3D Computer Vision, Generative AI, and Digital Humans. My work focuses on diffusion models, multimodal fusion, and human motion reconstruction — developing scalable pipelines that connect deep learning with geometry, simulation, and visual perception.

Currently, I am a Master’s student in Data Science at Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität Munich (LMU), completing my thesis on 3D latent diffusion avatars collaborating with Sony R&D in Stuttgart, where I work as a research intern. Previously, I worked at Adidas R&D on biomechanical data analysis and causal inference, and at IFCO Systems on optimization and production-level ML pipelines.
